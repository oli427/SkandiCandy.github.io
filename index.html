check out the list of delicios sweedish sweets and chocaltes you can buy below.

Dumle

The combination of the chewy caramel and the velvety chocolate creates a perfect balance of flavors and textures. Dumle Original offers a satisfying and indulgent treat that is enjoyed by both children and adults. The creamy caramel provides a sweet and buttery taste, while the milk chocolate adds a luxurious touch.

Daim

The Daim bar has a soft milk chocolate shell, which hides a hard caramel centre laced with almonds. It's hard enough that it can be snapped in half, and it crunches in your mouth before dissolving. It's got a buttery, milky, burnt sugar flavour to go with the toasted almond flakes. It's not bad at all.

Marabou

Milk Chocolate with Chopped Hazelnuts, Mint & Almond or Orange & Almond. Marabou has been the favourite chocolate brand in Sweden since it was launched in 1916.

japp

Japp consists of milk chocolate, filled with delicious cocoa meringue and soft caramel. It was launched in 1949 and is still a favorite among chocolate lovers.

polly

These delightfully chewy foam drops are flavoured with vanilla, arrack and butter toffee and coated in milk & dark chocolate. It's impossible to eat just one because of the incredible texture and flavers.

kex

KEX is the Swedish classic, which is eaten by 9 out of 10 Swedes. KEX is crispy waffle under a blanket of light milk chocolate. There's a broad selection of KEX sizes whether you'll have them for yourself or share with loved ones!

plopp

Cloetta Plopp are delicious milk chocolates, with a caramel filling. This one is a favourite, chocolate treat amongst the Swede's. Originally introduced in 1949, Plopp is personified by the little mini bar that stands for nostalgia, fun and playfulness.

tutti frutti

Tutti frutti (from Italian tutti i frutti, "all fruits"; also hyphenated tutti-frutti) is a colorful confectionery containing various chopped and usually candied fruits, or an artificial or natural flavouring simulating the combined flavour of many different fruits and vanilla.
